3D Printer

Duration: November 2017 - Today

For my birthday, I recieved a 3D printer kit, known as the Tevo Tarantula. During the following weeks, I began to build it, putting together the frame, then the bed and the gantry, and finally assembling and wiring everything together. This project helped to test my patience, as printing an object is not fast, and my ingenuity, as there were many problems along the way that also needed to be fixed. The reason this project has not ended yet is because I am still upgrading and modifying to this very day, to continue to make it safer and more accurate in printing. (Note the printed brackets to make it more secure)
